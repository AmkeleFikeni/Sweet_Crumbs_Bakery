About Us

Sweet Crumbs Bakery was founded in 2018 by Annabelle Collado, a self-taught baker with a passion for creating joy through desserts.

We believe in the value of homemade, crafted, quality baked goods. Not only do they warm the home, but our products will definitely warm the heart. Our favorite time of day is when our first batch comes out of the oven early in the morning, and the fresh smell fills the entire air. We like to keep it simple and emphasize taste over flash. Come check out what we’ve got prepared for you today. Your tummy will be happy and we know you will too.

Mission

To deliver not only delicious baked goods but also unforgettable moments of joy.

More About Sweet Crumbs Bakery

Sweet Crumbs Bakery is a community-inspired bakery specialising in handcrafted goods made with love, skill, and high-quality ingredients. Every recipe is created from scratch, tested in small batches, and perfected over time. We believe that baked goods should bring comfort, excitement, and a sense of home with every bite.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only premium, fresh, ethically sourced ingredients.
  • Creativity: Our menu is always growing with new inspirations and flavours.
  • Community: We proudly support local farms and small businesses.
  • Sustainability: Eco-friendly packaging and reduced food waste is a priority.
  • Authenticity: Every baked item is handmade — no shortcuts.

Why Customers Love Us

From warm breads to custom cakes and artisanal pastries, Sweet Crumbs offers a bakery experience unlike any other. Our loyal customers appreciate our attention to detail, warm hospitality, and the familiar flavours that remind them of home.

Our Promise

Sweet Crumbs promises freshness, flavour, and a delightful bakery experience every single day. Whether you’re buying a loaf, ordering a custom cake, or visiting for a sweet treat, we ensure quality and care in every crumb.
